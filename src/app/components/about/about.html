About Saksham

Founded by Akash Pandey, Saksham has become a trusted platform for online learning. We're dedicated to making quality education accessible to everyone, everywhere.

Our Mission

At Saksham, we believe that education is the key to unlocking human potential. Our mission is to provide accessible, high-quality online education that empowers individuals to achieve their career goals and personal aspirations.

Founded by Akash Pandey in 2020, Saksham has grown from a small startup to a global learning platform serving students in over 150 countries. We're committed to making education accessible, affordable, and effective for everyone.
